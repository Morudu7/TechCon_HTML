History

The history of Jotech techcon comes a long way. In 2002 on the 7th of October, Mr Thapelo Morudu founded Jotech techcon, with a dream of making young people of Johannesburg and surrounding areas to fully be aware of the potential, technology can have in their lives. The first Johtech conference was a succes, and during the conference over 150 businesses showcased their advanced technology and inovation to thee youth of Johannesburg.

Mission

Our mission at Jotech Techcon is to make young pepople of our country to fully aknowledge technology and AI in making their lives better. Jotech Techcon would also like to increase the number of tech companies participating in our conferences to over 2000 per conference in 2030.
